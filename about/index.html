About Us

To empower IIT Bombay students to break into Quantitative Finance by building clarity, skills, and real-world experience—together.

Quant @ IIT Bombay is a student-led initiative driven by curiosity, obsession with markets, and the hunger to solve real problems using math, code, and logic. We’re a tight-knit tribe of builders, learners, and tinkerers—from freshmen to final-years—who believe in deep work, shared momentum, and elite standards.

Purpose

Quant finance is confusing. No clear roadmap. No structured guidance. Too much noise online. We’re here to cut through the clutter—and build a high-trust system where any IITB student, regardless of branch or year, can go from zero to quant-ready.
